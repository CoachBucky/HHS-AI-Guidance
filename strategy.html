Harnessing AI for Student Success

Our strategic plan for integrating Artificial Intelligence to enhance learning and prepare students for the future.

1. Teaching with AI

Using AI as a powerful tool to support daily instruction.

  • Content Creation: Teachers generate story starters, custom images, and interactive games.
  • Personalized Practice: Adaptive platforms provide targeted math and reading practice.
  • Differentiation: Quickly create leveled reading passages and modified assignments.
  • Brainstorming Partner: Students use AI to generate ideas and outlines for projects.
  • Skill Reinforcement: Get instant feedback on writing, grammar, and math problems.
  • Efficient Assessment: Teachers create quizzes and rubrics to save time for instruction.
  • Advanced Research: Analyze data sets, summarize complex articles, and explore perspectives.
  • Creative & Technical Skills: Use generative AI for coding assistance, graphic design, and problem-solving.
  • Personalized Feedback: AI acts as a "tutor" for detailed feedback on complex assignments.

2. Teaching about AI

Integrating AI literacy into the curriculum for responsible use.

  • AI Awareness: Identify "smart" devices and understand they follow instructions.
  • Digital Safety: Learn the basics of online safety and not sharing personal info.
  • How AI Works: Introduce core concepts like algorithms and machine learning.
  • Ethics and Bias: Discuss how data can lead to unfair outcomes and the importance of fairness.
  • AI Ethics & Responsibility: Cover data privacy, ethics, and identifying AI-generated content.
  • AI and the Future: Explore the impact of AI on career fields and develop collaborative skills.

3. Supporting Our Teachers

Committing to our educators' professional growth with AI.

  • Foundational Training: Provide hands-on training on district-approved AI platforms for practical classroom applications.
  • Collaborative Learning: Use AI-enhanced platforms to share best practices, lesson plans, and effective prompts among staff.
  • Personalized Support: Offer personalized training resources that align with each educator's specific needs and career goals.

Our Commitment to Responsible AI

✓ Data Privacy and Security: Implementing robust measures to protect all student information and comply with regulations like FERPA.

✓ Ethical Vendor Selection: Thoroughly vetting all AI programs to ensure they meet our high standards for safety, privacy, and inclusivity.

✓ Ongoing Review: Continuously monitoring our AI systems for bias and reviewing security protocols to maintain trust and safety.
